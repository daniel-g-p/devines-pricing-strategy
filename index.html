Subscription Term

Subscription should run on a year-by-year basis, but billing should occur monthly.

Plan Upgrades

Customers should be free to upgrade their plan midway through a subscription term if they wish to do so. Such upgrades shall come at the usual price and shall be charged on a pro-rata basis for the remainder of the term.

Warranty & Insurance

We should give customers an option to purchase both a warranty and/or an insurance for a yearly premium.

Product Testing

New customers should be eligible for a 3-month testing phase, during which they will be able to test DeVines with 1 receiver and 5GB of cloud storage for a total of €1500.

Maintenance and Repairs

Customers shall always receive all software updates and should be granted one hardware revision every year.

Price Strategy

We should launch DeVines with a price skimming approach, starting at a fairly high price to optimize the technology with few customers. Further down the line, as production efficiency and possibly economies of scale increase, we could lower the price to target a larger segment and internationalize the service.

Promotional Strategy

Firstly, we should launch an afiliate program for DeVines as viticulture is a very community-driven industry. Secondly, we should incentivize immediate orders with promotional offers, for example offering a discount code valid for one month once a potential customer completes the pricing guide for the first time. And lastly, we should keep doing individual consultation the way we are doing it now as we offer a high-ticket service.

Distribution Strategy

We should exclusively sell DeVines through our website. For that, we need to implement eCommerce functionality into the website as soon as possible.
